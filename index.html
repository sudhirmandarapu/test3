About Me

I grew up in Kenya and Australia before coming to Canada where I completed my undergraduate degree in Mechanical Engineering with a Mathematics Minor from the University of Alberta. I am currently in my second year of graduate studies where the objective of my research project is to develop a system-scale finite element model of the adhesive layer used in add-on vehicle armors under impact.

I am very passionate about tall ships, specifically building ship models of the 1700 to 1800 period. Some of my works (ongoing and completed) are presented below. Each of these models require well over 1000 hours to complete. A lot of patience, problem solving, woodworking/craftsmanship skills, and attention to detail is essential to dive into these rewarding projects. Please feel free to check them out!
